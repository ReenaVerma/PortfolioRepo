about reena

A Full Stack Developer, with 10 years experience in Digital, spanning across Website Production, Content Migrations, SEO, Optimisation and CMS export in (Sitecore, Adobe AEM, Typo 3, Tridion SDL). Strong UX, Design, Analytics, Agile and Line Management skills.

I decided to join General Assembly’s WDI, which I knew would give me the skills and tools I’d need to change career. And most importantly, to spend the rest of my life growing my career in a field, that I’m really interested in… web development.
